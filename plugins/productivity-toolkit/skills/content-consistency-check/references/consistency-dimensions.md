# Consistency Dimensions

- **Terminology:** preferred term, aliases, capitalization, translation, acronym expansion.
- **Figures:** value, unit, scale, precision, sign, denominator, reporting period, formula.
- **Dates:** event date, publication date, effective date, timezone, locale, calendar.
- **People and entities:** exact name, title, role, organization, effective period.
- **Versions:** document version, product release, policy edition, schema revision.
- **Links and citations:** target, anchor, identifier, access status, cited claim.
- **Conclusions:** scope, evidence basis, confidence, recommendation, effective date.

Normalize for comparison only. A convertible or differently formatted value is not automatically a conflict.
