# Basic Word Document Review

Review a Word document using generic, public-safe checks.

## Safety Boundary

- Do not use company-internal templates.
- Do not provide legal conclusions.
- Do not rewrite the document unless explicitly asked.
- Cite paragraph, heading, table, or section references where possible.

## Workflow

1. Identify document structure and intended purpose.
2. Check heading hierarchy, numbering, tables, references, and missing sections.
3. Identify inconsistent terminology and unresolved placeholders.
4. Cite evidence for each finding.
5. List manual confirmation items.
