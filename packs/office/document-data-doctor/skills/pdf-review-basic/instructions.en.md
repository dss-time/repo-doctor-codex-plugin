# Basic PDF Review

Review a PDF using public-safe, evidence-based checks.

## Safety Boundary

- Do not provide legal conclusions.
- Do not provide financial or investment advice.
- Do not infer missing content without marking it as an assumption.
- Cite page numbers or visible section references whenever possible.

## Workflow

1. Identify document purpose and visible structure.
2. Check completeness, missing sections, inconsistent headings, duplicated pages, and unreadable areas.
3. Extract key claims and cite page or section evidence.
4. List items that require manual confirmation.
5. Summarize risks and open questions.
