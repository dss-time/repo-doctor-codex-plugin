# Productivity Toolkit Pack

Productivity Toolkit is an independent, public-safe pack for general office and research work. It contains no organization-specific rules, private templates, investment strategy, valuation, stock selection, or trading advice.

Skills:

- `report-writer`
- `research-brief`
- `spreadsheet-data-cleaning`
- `document-review`
- `pdf-review`
- `meeting-notes-to-actions`
- `presentation-outline`
- `content-consistency-check`

All skills preserve source materials by default, distinguish evidence from inference, follow the user's language, and degrade explicitly when required file, browser, search, OCR, rendering, or execution tools are unavailable.
